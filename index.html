Key Insights
Treatment gap
50.7% of people with high mood swings are still untreated — the single largest unmet care need in this dataset.
Gender disparity
Women are 23 percentage points more likely to seek treatment (69.4% vs 46.4%) despite representing only 11.7% of respondents.
Care access gap
41.7% have no care options and 26.1% are unsure — meaning only 1 in 3 respondents has confirmed access to mental health support.
Social withdrawal
41% of respondents spent between 1 month and 2+ months indoors, signalling widespread social isolation and withdrawal.
Stress near-universal
Only 31.5% report no increasing stress — nearly 7 in 10 are experiencing or possibly experiencing escalating stress.
Hereditary risk
38% report a family history of mental illness — a consistent hereditary risk factor present across all occupation groups in the dataset.
